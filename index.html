Who I Am

Hi! My name is Brianna Pacheco owner and LMT of New Age Massage. I’ve been married for 12 years and have 2 beautiful children. People always ask why I chose this profession. My response is very simple, I love helping others who are hurting or who want to relax the soul. I’ve been in the massage industry for over 5 years and been on my own for 1 of those years. Within these past 5 years I have developed and refined myself as a therapist. I try very hard to individually cater to every client I work on, and listen to what they want and grow with what they need. Because without growth there wouldn’t be a “New Age” where massage therapist evolve and improve. I’m grateful for you to have took the next step to ultimate relaxation and hope to see you soon.

Who We Are

OUR PHILOSOPHY... “New Age” represents a New Age of thinking for all massage therapist, where massage is evolving and catering to the clients’ needs. We should break out of traditional massage to include our client’s opinions and our own. Never being afraid to experiment with new ideas and techniques

Deep Tissue Massage

30 min - 120min Massage.

Aims to relieve tension in the deeper layers of tissue in the body. Deep tissue massage is a highly effective method for releasing chronic stress areas due to misalignment, repetitive motions, and past lingering injuries. Using forearms and elbows and fist techniques, discomfort may be felt during this massage. Communication on how much pressure will be acknowledged during massage. Firm to very deep pressure is used.
